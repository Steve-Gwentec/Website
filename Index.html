Company Description

Gwentec provide calibration and automation services to industry. Established as a partnership in 1992 we became Gwentec Ltd in 2008.

We provide a variety of services including traceable on and off site calibration, instrument and control system design and manufacture and refurbishment or repair of existing control equipment.

Our trained and experienced staff provide a knowledgeable and timely service for all of your automation and calibration requirements.

We offer traceable calibration services using qualified staff and written procedures for a wide variety of measurement and control equipment.

Temperature, Gauge Pressure, Absolute Pressure, Humidity, Flow, Level , Ph, Conductivity, RPM, Timers, Volts, Amps Torque, and many others.

Calibration services are available both on and off site for most types of industry but we specialise particularly in Pharmaceutical and Pharma related companies. We can provide copies of procedures and traceable test equipment certificates together with staff training records. Assistance with, or authoring of Technical Agreements including all site instrument calibration details is a service we can provide with site calibration contacts. We provide a transparent, detailed and reliable service that has assisted many of our customers during audits from their clients and regulatory bodies.

 

South Wales Hanwell Distributor

Hanwell units are wireless battery powered environmental monitoring. These units transmit their readings to a base station which can then send data to a local server PC on site or to an external cloud based server managed by Hanwell. Users can then monitor live data and look at historical data using any device capable of running a modern browser (eg: the device you’re currently viewing this website on).

The system can be setup to trigger local alarms when a transmitter records readings that are outside user specified limits. The system can also send out emails on alarms or even SMS text messages (additional hardware required for SMS feature).

The system can be set to automatically produce reports of selected groups of transmitters within a particular area (a warehouse for example). The report can be scheduled to print out at the same time each week.

If you have a large site and would like environmental monitoring then we can help. We not only supply Hanwell wireless loggers but we also install and calibrate the units on site. We can also offer technical support and training as we have worked with the Hanwell units and software in PCI Tredegar since 2011.
